Junior Data Analyst

Data, Analytics & Insight

Description

Job Title: Junior Data Analyst

Location: Toronto | Job Type: Full-Time | Experience: 6 months – 1 year

About the Role

We are looking for a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Data Analyst to join our growing team in the advertising industry. The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in data handling and a curiosity for uncovering insights that drive business decisions. While prior experience in advertising is a plus, it’s not required — we welcome candidates from diverse industry backgrounds who are eager to learn and grow.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in collecting, cleaning, and organizing large datasets from various sources.
  • Perform exploratory data analysis and identify trends, patterns, and anomalies.
  • Support campaign performance analysis and regular reporting tasks.
  • Collaborate with internal teams to understand data needs and deliver insights.
  • Help maintain documentation for data processes and definitions.

Required Qualifications

  • 6 months to 1 year of hands-on experience working with data in any industry.
  • Proficiency in Excel/Google Sheets for data analysis.
  • Basic knowledge of SQL or any data querying language.
  • Familiarity with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI, or similar).
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Good communication skills and ability to explain data findings to non-technical stakeholders.

 

Nice to Have

  • Exposure to marketing or advertising data (e.g., campaign metrics, media performance).
  • Understanding of digital media platforms (e.g., Google Ads, Meta, DSPs).
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment.

Why Join Us

  • Opportunity to work in a dynamic, creative industry with a data-driven focus.
  • Learn from experienced analysts and marketers.
  • Access to real-world advertising datasets and tools.
  • Career development support and training opportunities.
